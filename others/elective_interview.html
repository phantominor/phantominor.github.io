7. 介紹台灣的衛生健康政策
參考答案：
Taiwan's health policies are centered around its National Health Insurance (NHI) system, implemented in 1995.

The good thing is: thanks to the low costs of our health insurance, over 99% of the population is insured in Taiwan, and most of the people can have high quality medical service with the price of a cup of simple beverage.

Nevertheless, the accessibility and cheapness also lead to overcrowded hospitals and a decline in the quality of life of medical staff. Now we are trying to seek a new balance between the price and quality of our National Health Insurance system.
8. 台灣的轉診制度運作如何？ referral system
參考答案：
Taiwan's referral system aims to optimize healthcare resource allocation:
  1. Primary care: Patients are encouraged to first visit local clinics or community hospitals for common ailments
  2. Referrals: If needed, primary care doctors refer patients to regional hospitals or medical centers for specialized care
  3. Incentives: The NHI provides financial incentives for patients who follow the referral system
  4. Flexibility: Patients can still directly access higher-level care, but with higher co-payment
  5. Information sharing: A national electronic health record system facilitates smooth transitions between levels of care
While the system aims to reduce overcrowding at large hospitals, there's ongoing work to improve its effectiveness and public adherence.
